Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a yacht charter?
Our yacht charters include a professional crew, fuel, and basic amenities. Additional services and catering can be arranged upon request.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak season. However, we can accommodate last-minute bookings subject to availability.
What is the cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 14 days or more before the charter date receive a full refund. Cancellations within 14 days are subject to a 50% cancellation fee.
